Abiri Bankole, popularly known as Bankyondbeatz is a 23 year old music producer born in Lagos state, Nigeria. The graduate of Business Administration from the prestigious Babcock University who ventured into music production in 2010, joined forces with another talented producer GMK, and 2 buzzing new school rappers - Ozzy B and Retrodee and together they formed a formidable movement called Monster Boys. Bankyondbeatz is a name well associated with notable figures in the industry like Niniola, Muna, Tomi Thomas, Reminisce, BOJ of DRB Lasgidi, Flowssick, Rayce, and more. With a large production catalogue including his 2 bodies of work - Legendary Reflections the mixtape - which was labelled by Pulse.ng as ‘a huge production masterpiece’ and Fuego Senoras – a groundbreaking all-female EP, the producer extraordinaire has carved a niche for himself in the Nigerian music underground digital space as one of the leaders of the new school sound.
